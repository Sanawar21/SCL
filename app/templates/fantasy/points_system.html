Special Ball Rules

Wide ball:

Bowler gets -2 base penalty. If extra wides include boundary component, additional bowling penalties are added where applicable.

No ball:

Bowler gets -4 base penalty and boundary/run penalties can be added from runs off the bat.

Valid ball count:

Only valid balls increase bowler ball count. Batter ball count excludes wides.

Role Multipliers

Positive batting points by a player with role BOWLER get 1.2x.

Positive bowling points by a player with role BATTER get 1.2x.

Final Score Formula

Total points = batting points + bowling points

Total balls = balls faced + balls bowled

Divisor = max(total balls, 6)

PPB = total points / divisor

Final score = PPB x 12
